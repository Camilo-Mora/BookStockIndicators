Basic trading indicators

5 Price Indicators

Another way to get insights into stock trends, is what is called technical analysis. Basically, trying to get insights from statistical trends on different indexes used to measure/feel the stock.

There are tens of such indexes measuring different attributes (e.g., price, volume or both) and trying to assess different aspects of the market.

Many people develop their own indexes, hoping to find the magic index that will tell them, when to buy and when to sell.

Certainly, people and companies that are successful at trading, probably have their own index or combination of them; as you may image having a good index could make all the difference.

Lets review some broadly used indexes and what tell us.
