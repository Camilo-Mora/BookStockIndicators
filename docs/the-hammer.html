Basic trading indicators

The hammer

This pattern involves a single candle stick at the end of a bearish trend. That candle looks just like a hammer….a long shadown and a smaller body.

It does not matter whether it is bull or bear, but it has to be at the end of a downward price trend.

What the candle tell you is that there was a massive attempt by the bear to bring down the price (Evidednce by the low shadow of the candle), but when closing the price when still up. Basically the attack during that time was not enought for the bear to win the battle deccisibly.

The consequence of the anemic effort is for the longers to dominate and win. It is expected for the price to grow after a hammer candle.

Figure 5.18: The hammer

The hammer can also also be inverted and have the same effect. The price will go up.
